🧬 Life of an IITM BS Student

⏰Flexible Routine

Subah utho ya jab mann kare tab utho – no fixed schedule! You’re your own boss 🧘‍♂️

📦Weekly Workload

Ek baar mein ek week ka kaam milta hai. Usey deadline se pehle complete karke submit karo ✔️

🎥Live Sessions

Live sessions attend karna optional hai, but bahut helpful hote hain doubt clear karne ke liye 🧠

📝Monthly Quizzes

Har mahine ya kisi bhi din (usually Sunday) TCS iON center par jaake Quiz 1 ya Quiz 2 do. Dono mein se ek dena compulsory hai!

📚End Term Exam

Endterm dena zaroori hai final course grade ke liye. Exam ka hall ticket tabhi milega jab 12 week mein se 10 week ke assignment score 40% se zyada ho.

🎥Practical Exams (OPPE)

Kuch subjects ke liye OPPE (Open Book Practical Exam) hota hai — video call ke through, ghar se hi strict rules ke saath exam conduct hota hai.

🏆Course Grading

Har subject ka grading pattern alag hota hai — assignment, quizzes, endterm & OPPE ke performance se final grade banta hai 📊
